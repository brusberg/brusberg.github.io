Computer Science Club

Let's Make Ideas Real

Computer Science Club started when a good friend of mine started a conversation that lasted long after school closed about computer science and asked me to teach him how to code. Stayying after school and knowing the computer science teacher was still dowing work in her room, we decided to stop by. We would then end up stayying after school untill 5 or 6 Monday through Thursday and soon more people came to learn how to program and work on projects. Friends or strangers, it did not matter because we all bonded over pizza and code. The club became official when we could not afford pizza for 10 to 15 people and we realized we could get half off pizza at most pizzerias in town because they offer half off pizza for Sparta clubs.
